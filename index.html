Privacy Policy — Little Lamb Disciples

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Little Lamb Disciples (“the App”) is a Bible-story game for young children, developed by Tony Christopher Giordano (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We take children’s privacy seriously. This policy explains, in plain language, how the App handles information. The short version: the App does not collect any data.

We do not collect any personal information

The App does not collect, store, use, transmit, or share any personal information about you or your child. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in. The App requires no registration, login, name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • No tracking. We do not track users across apps or websites. The App contains no advertising and no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • No usage analytics. We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, device identifiers, or crash data.
  • No location data. The App does not request or access location.
  • No contacts, photos, camera, or microphone. The App does not access the microphone, camera, photo library, contacts, or any similar device data.

Everything runs on the device

All of the App’s content — images, narration audio, and Bible-story activities — is included in the App itself and runs entirely on your device. The App does not require an internet connection to play, and it does not send any information off the device.

The App uses your device’s built-in text-to-speech voice (Apple’s on-device speech feature) to read words aloud. This processing happens on the device and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Made for children

Little Lamb Disciples is designed for children and is intended to comply with the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple’s requirements for the Kids category. Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it collects no personal information from children.

  • There are no in-app purchases.
  • There is no advertising.
  • There are no social features, chat, or user-generated content.
  • There are no links that lead children out of the App.

No data sharing

Because we collect no data, we have no data to sell, rent, or share with third parties, and we do not do so.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the App handles information, we will update this policy and revise the “Effective date” above. Material changes will be reflected in an App update.
